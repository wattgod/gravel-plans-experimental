**Course Layout:**
- Starts: Historic downtown Beaver
- Finishes: Eagle Point Resort upper lodge (10,500 ft elevation)
- Mix of slow and extreme climbs/descents
- Five fully stocked aid stations
- Multiple terrain types

**Specific Sections:**

**Miles 41-46: Piute Valley pavement**

**Miles 61-65: Benenson Flat approach**

**Miles 65-69.9: Final climb to Eagle Point**
