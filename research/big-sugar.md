### Tire Strategy:
- Wider tires (44-50mm) for loose/sharp gravel
- Knobby tread beneficial (not pure slick)
- File tread acceptable
- Lower pressure: 28-32 PSI (absorb sharp rocks, traction)

### Bike Setup:

### Mechanical Prep:
