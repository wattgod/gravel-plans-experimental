## Race-Specific Features

### Non-Negotiable Training Challenges

1. **Weather adaptation training** (Weeks 4-10)
   - Unpredictable conditions: 40-75°F swings, freezing rain, heat, wildfires
   - Cold weather, heat, and wind training protocols

2. **Red clay handling skills** (Weeks 6-8)
   - Oklahoma red clay becomes unrideable mud when wet
   - Bike handling and mud preparation

3. **Wind exposure training** (Weeks 7-9)
   - Exposed ridgelines amplify wind
   - Tactical pack racing preparation

4. **Dress rehearsal** (Week 9)
   - 4-7 hours depending on tier
   - Tests nutrition, pacing, and mental preparation

### Mid South-Specific Content
